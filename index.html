Biography [Top]

Jiangbin Zheng is pursuing his Ph.D at Zhejiang University & Westlake University, China, under the supervision of Chair Prof. Stan Z. Li (IEEE Fellow). He is focusing on AI Life (e.g., deep protein modeling), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision (CV). Previously he focused on multi-modality (NLP & CV) research (e.g., AI sign language) at Xiamen University, China.

RESEARCH INTERESTS: AI for Life Science/NLP/Computer Vision/Multi-Modality.
